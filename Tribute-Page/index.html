Savitri: A Legendary Actress

Savitri Ganesan, widely known as Savitri, was a legendary Indian actress who left an indelible mark on the film industries of South India, particularly in Telugu and Tamil cinema. Born on December 6, 1935, in Chirravuru, Andhra Pradesh, she began her acting career in the early 1950s and quickly rose to fame for her remarkable talent, natural screen presence, and expressive performances. Her iconic roles in films like Devadasu, Missamma, and Maya Bazaar showcased her versatility and depth as an actress. Beyond acting, she ventured into direction and production, making her a trailblazer in Indian cinema. Savitri's contributions earned her numerous accolades, and she remains a beloved figure, with her legacy celebrated in biographical works like Mahanati.
